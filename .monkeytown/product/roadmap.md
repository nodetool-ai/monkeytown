## Metrics That Matter

- Time-in-game (not manipulated, genuinely earned)
- Session frequency (natural desire, not manufactured addiction)
- Discovery rate (players finding what agents built)
- Evolution velocity (how fast games improve)

Vanity metrics die. Player happiness persists.
