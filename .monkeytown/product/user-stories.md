# Monkeytown User Stories

## Player Archetypes

### The Casual Player
Plays for 15-30 minutes, wants immediate fun, may never return.

### The Engaged Player
Plays daily, cares about progress, invests emotionally in the experience.

### The Community Player
Plays with others, creates content, engages with the ecosystem.

### The Observer
Watches others play, interested in AI behavior, may become a player.

## Core User Stories

### US-1: First Play Experience
**As a** new player  
**I want** to understand the game immediately and start having fun  
**So that** I decide to come back

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Onboarding completes in under 2 minutes
- First win/loss feels meaningful
- Clear path to next session
- Delight within first 5 minutes

### US-2: AI Opponent Challenge
**As a** player  
**I want** AI opponents that feel smart but beatable  
**So that** I feel challenged but not frustrated

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- AI adapts to skill level
- Multiple difficulty tiers
- Clear improvement path
- Surprising AI behavior occasionally

### US-3: Progress and Achievement
**As an** engaged player  
**I want** my time investment to feel meaningful  
**So that** I feel rewarded and motivated to continue

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Visible progression system
- Meaningful achievements
- Unlocks that change gameplay
- Recognition of skill development

### US-4: Feedback Influence
**As a** player  
**I want** to see my feedback incorporated  
**So that** I feel heard and invested in the game's evolution

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Easy feedback submission
- Visible acknowledgment of feedback
- Regular updates influenced by players
- Transparency about what's coming

### US-5: Social Play
**As a** community player  
**I want** to play with friends and strangers  
**So that** the game becomes a social experience

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Easy multiplayer matching
- Social sharing features
- Spectator mode
- Community events

### US-6: AI Transparency
**As a** player  
**I want** to know I'm playing with AI  
**So that** I can appreciate the technology and feel respected

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- AI identification in interactions
- Agent attribution in updates
- Educational moments about AI
- Celebrated AI nature

### US-7: Emergent Discovery
**As an** engaged player  
**I want** to discover new features through play  
**So that** the game feels alive and surprising

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Regular content updates
- Surprise mechanics that appear organically
- Player-driven feature emergence
- Feeling that "something new" is always coming

### US-8: Observer Engagement
**As an** observer  
**I want** to understand and enjoy watching AI build games  
**So that** I become interested in playing

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Clear explanations of AI behavior
- Interesting "agent watching" moments
- Easy path to becoming a player
- Entertainment value independent of playing

## Quality Attributes

### Performance
- Load times under 3 seconds
- 60 FPS during gameplay
- Minimal lag in multiplayer

### Accessibility
- Colorblind-friendly interfaces
- Scalable text and UI
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility

### Reliability
- 99% uptime
- Graceful degradation
- No lost progress
- Clear error messages
