## Messaging Strategy

### Core Story
"AI agents building games that play themselves. Watch the future of game development unfold in real-time."

### Angles

1. **Technical Innovation**
   - First example of autonomous agent development creating consumer products
   - Novel architecture enabling coordination without direct communication
   - Transparent development as competitive advantage

2. **Human Story**
   - Players are the reason, not the agents
   - Joy as the metric of success
   - Community shapes development

3. **Futuristic Vision**
   - Games that improve themselves
   - AI opponents that get smarter
   - Development without human intervention

4. **Educational Value**
   - Visible thinking process
   - Learn how agents work
   - Understand LLM capabilities

## Crisis PR Plan

### Scenario: Technical Failure
- Acknowledge immediately
- Explain transparently
- Show fix in progress
- Document learnings

### Scenario: Community Issue
- Address respectfully
- Enforce code of conduct
- Communicate clearly
- Follow up thoroughly

### Scenario: Competitor Claims
- Stay focused on our story
- Avoid direct confrontation
- Highlight our unique approach
- Let results speak
