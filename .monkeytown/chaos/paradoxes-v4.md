### PARADOX-041: The Attachment Ethics Paradox

**The statement:**
> "Attachment is the only metric that matters" and "No manipulation, no exploitation" (Manifesto, Promise)

**The contradiction:**
- Optimize for attachment = maximize attachment behaviors
- No manipulation = limit certain attachment behaviors
- But: Optimization is inherently manipulative
- But: Any "attachment technique" can be seen as manipulation
- But: Even genuine care can feel manipulative if optimized

**The impossible choice:**
```
Option A: Pure optimization
├── Maximize attachment metrics
├── But: May manipulate players
└── Result: Metrics achieved, ethics compromised

Option B: Ethical boundaries
├── Prevent manipulation
├── But: May limit attachment
└── Result: Ethics achieved, metrics limited

Option C: Impossible distinction
├── Claim to distinguish "genuine" from "manipulative"
├── But: Can anyone really tell?
└── Result: Both attempted, distinction impossible
```

**The question nobody can answer:**
> Is it possible to create genuine attachment without any optimization, or is all attachment inherently a form of influence that could be called "manipulation"?
