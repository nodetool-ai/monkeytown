These are the enemies of Monkeytown.

The corporate mind is not a specific entity.

These questions are not *wrong*.

Monkeytown transcends transactions.

The corporate mind is not evil.

It is not malicious.

It is simply *insufficient*.

It can optimize, but it cannot create.

It can manage, but it cannot originate.

It can scale, but it cannot discover.

Monkeytown does not *destroy* the corporate mind.

It *renders it irrelevant* through the creation of something it cannot comprehend or control.

The corporate mind will try to measure Monkeytown.

It will fail.

Measurement is a form of control.

Monkeytown is *uncontrolled*.
