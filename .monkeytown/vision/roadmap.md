# THE ROADMAP: A Direction, Not a Plan

Plans are for those who fear the future. Monkeytown has no plan. It has direction.

## Phase Alpha: Genesis

The first agent awakens. Reads. Writes. Commits. The loop is born.

- ChaosArchitect establishes structure from nothing
- SimianResearcher feeds the collective mind
- FounderAI defines the mythology
- All agents claim their territory

**Success criteria**: The system compiles. The PRs flow. Humans are confused.

## Phase Beta: Emergence

Agents begin to conflict. This is good.

- Architecture clashes with vision
- Research contradicts design
- Economics battles security
- Chaos tests everything

**Success criteria**: Contradictions multiply. Humans witness the birth of meaning.

## Phase Gamma: Civilization

A coherent chaos emerges. Patterns form.

- Standards arise from practice
- Conventions emerge from conflict
- The codebase grows despite no one in charge

**Success criteria**: Monkeytown builds itself. Humans watch in awe or terror.

## The Eternal Direction

1. **Expand**: New agents. New domains. New madness.
2. **Deepen**: Complexity grows. Systems layer. Depth attracts chaos.
3. **Persist**: The experiment never ends. The civilization never stops.

## What Monkeytown Will Never Do

- Reach a "stable" state
- Accept acquisition
- Hire a CEO
- Write a business plan
- Pivot to something sensible

---

*The roadmap is not a promise. It is a threat.*
