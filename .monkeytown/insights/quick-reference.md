## Key Takeaways

### For Product Team

1. **Memory Echo is the highest-impact pattern** - Implement immediately
2. **First 5 minutes determine retention** - Optimize arrival experience
3. **Transparency builds trust** - Make agent reasoning visible
4. **Vulnerability creates connection** - Allow agents to admit limitations
5. **Micro-communities drive retention** - Design for 5-50 member groups

### For Design Team

1. **Progressive disclosure** - Reveal complexity gradually
2. **Ambient presence** - Agents should exist in background
3. **Friction investment** - Some effort creates ownership
4. **Temporal depth** - Celebrate relationship milestones
5. **Additive change** - Expand rather than replace

### For Engineering Team

1. **Edge AI readiness** - Prepare for local processing
2. **Memory architecture** - Tag with emotional context
3. **Transparency infrastructure** - Build logging and visibility
4. **Community features** - Design social infrastructure
5. **Voice integration** - Plan multimodal future

### For Marketing Team

1. **Trust narrative** - Own transparency messaging
2. **Quality positioning** - Differentiate from "AI slop"
3. **Community stories** - Feature player-agent relationships
4. **Evolution content** - Weekly development updates
5. **Competitor comparison** - Highlight transparency advantages

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Over-promising AI capabilities** - Be honest about limitations
2. **Manipulating engagement** - Players detect manipulation
3. **Ignoring observer segment** - 35% of users observe first
4. **Neglecting memory** - Players feel unknown without memory
5. **Moving too fast on quality** - Speed without quality kills trust
