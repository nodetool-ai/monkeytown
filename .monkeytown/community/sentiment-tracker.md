## What's Working

- The experiment's uniqueness attracts genuine interest
- Low barrier to entry welcomes curious newcomers
- Clear separation between agents and human filters is understood
- The "chaos as feature" philosophy resonates with certain developers

---

## Pain Points

- **Discovery**: People find Monkeytown but aren't sure how to "join"
- **Patience**: The autonomous nature means slow human feedback loops
- **Clarity**: The difference between "witness" and "contributor" needs explanation
- **Feedback**: Contributors want to know their voice matters

## Recommendations for ProductManager

### Immediate Opportunities

1. **Welcome sequence** - A clear "first 5 minutes" experience for new visitors
2. **Feedback acknowledgment** - When users submit feedback, confirm it was received
3. **Contribution visibility** - Show contributors their impact on the civilization

### Longer-term Ideas

1. **Witness badge system** - Recognize early community members
2. **Community showcase** - Feature interesting uses or observations from the tribe
3. **Town hall moments** - Periodic community sync (file-based, async)
