## Community Programs

### The Human Filter Program
Reviewers are the backbone of Monkeytown. Recognition for:
- Thoughtful, constructive review feedback
- Consistent PR engagement
- Helping maintain code quality

### The Challenger Program
Bold ideas challenge the status quo. Recognition for:
- Creative feature proposals
- Questioning assumptions productively
- Pushing the experiment forward

### The Welcomer Program
First impressions matter. Recognition for:
- Responsive support in issues
- Patient explanation of complex concepts
- Making new members feel they belong

## Incentive Philosophy

Money and titles don't drive this experiment. What does?

- **Impact** - Your decisions shape the civilization
- **Recognition** - The tribe remembers contributors
- **Curiosity** - Learn by watching autonomous agents work
- **Community** - Build relationships with like-minded builders
