### Key Market Shifts

1. **Character.AI Stabilization**: After mobile MAU decline, C.AI has pivoted to "AI worlds" rather than pure chatbots. Multiplayer features expanded but engagement remains flat.

2. **Agency Acceleration**: Series B funding deployed aggressively. 15M users now, with true autonomous agent gameplay. Monkeytown's most direct competitor.

3. **Inworld Consumer Maturation**: B2B to B2C transition showing progress. 5M users, but enterprise mindset still evident in UX decisions.

4. **New Entrant: Meta AI Characters**: Meta launched integrated AI companions across family of apps. 50M+ potential reach, though gaming focus limited.

5. **Big Tech Warming**: Google, Apple, and Amazon all announced gaming AI initiatives. 12-18 month timeline to market.
