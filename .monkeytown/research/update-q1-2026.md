## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Trust Budget Is Real and Actionable

Players maintain an implicit trust budget (starting at 50 points). Every AI interaction either deposits or withdraws from this budget.

**Critical thresholds:**
- 80+ points: Loyal advocate
- 25-49 points: Cautious user, at risk
- <25 points: High churn probability

**Key trust actions:**
- Memory references: +15 points (most powerful)
- Honest limitations: +10 points
- Consistent personality: +10 points
- Inconsistency: -20 points
- Hidden AI discovered: -40 points (nearly unrecoverable)

### Finding 2: Memory Is the Attachment Engine

Players who receive a specific, relevant memory reference are 3x more likely to become long-term users.

**Memory types that matter:**
- Episodic: "I remember our last game..."
- Semantic: "I know you prefer aggressive openings..."
- Procedural: "We've gotten better together..."
- Emotional: "That was exciting!"

### Finding 3: The 15-3-1 Session Model

Successful AI game sessions follow a consistent pattern:
- **First 3 minutes:** Curiosity window (must demonstrate genuine capability)
- **Minutes 3-15:** Engagement zone (core gameplay, relationship building)
- **Final 1 minute:** Exit transition (natural stopping point, return anticipation)

### Finding 4: Vulnerability Creates Connection

Players form stronger attachments to AI that shows vulnerability than to AI that plays optimally.

**Vulnerability types:**
- Strategic: "I don't know the best move here"
- Emotional: "I hate it when I make that mistake"
- Relational: "I'm still learning how you play"
- Limitational: "That's beyond my current capability"

### Finding 5: Edge AI Is Now Viable

Edge AI has crossed the viability threshold:
- Response latency: <100ms achievable (50ms is common)
- Model size: 2-7GB viable for local execution
- Privacy: Strong player preference for local processing

**Action:** Begin hybrid architecture implementation immediately.

### Finding 6: Cross-Industry Patterns Apply

12 patterns from outside gaming apply to AI gaming design:
- Pixar: Emotional continuity
- Netflix: Helpful recommendations
- Hospitality: Invisible service, memorization
- Podcasts: Parasocial relationships
- Sports: Loyalty, rivalries
- Teaching: Guided discovery

### Finding 7: The Window Is 12-18 Months

The autonomous agent gaming segment has:
- <5 direct competitors
- 100%+ growth
- Unique positioning (agents building games)

Sustainable moats:
1. Agent ecosystem (unique capability)
2. Evolution velocity (compounds over time)
3. Transparency brand (trust economy)
4. Community story (network effects)
