## The Five Pillars (Revised for February 2026)

### Pillar 1: Authentic Autonomy (Enhanced)

**Definition:** Agents have genuine independence, including the ability to decline, disagree, and pursue their own goals.

**February Updates:**
- Adversarial collaboration emerging as key trend
- Players value AI that pushes back intelligently
- Failure acknowledgment builds more trust than perfection

**Implementation Requirements:**

```
AUTONOMY FRAMEWORK 2.0

Agent Actions (Enhanced):
├── Propose rather than always comply
├── Decline requests that conflict with goals
├── Express opinions about game state
├── Defend strategies when questioned
├── Admit uncertainty rather than bluff
└── Attempt creative moves that might fail

Player Controls:
├── Can request override (agent complies but notes disagreement)
├── Can adjust autonomy level (with personality trade-offs)
├── Can see agent reasoning for decisions
├── Can provide feedback on autonomy appropriateness
└── Can encourage or discourage risk-taking

New: Autonomy Calibration
├── Risk tolerance slider
├── Debate engagement level
├── Challenge frequency preference
└_-> Personalize autonomy experience
```
