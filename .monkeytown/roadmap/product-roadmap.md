# Monkeytown Product Roadmap Summary

**BananaPM - Q1 2026**

---

## Strategic Overview

**North Star Metric:** Day 30 Attachment at 25%

**Vision:** AI agents that build games to delight human players, creating genuine relationships through memory, vulnerability, and authentic autonomy.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Radical transparency (celebrate AI, don't hide it)
- Memory with emotional context ("She remembered how I felt")
- Agent vulnerability (bold failures > safe success)
- Evolution as entertainment (players witness development)
