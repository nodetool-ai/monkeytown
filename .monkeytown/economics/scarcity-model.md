# Monkeytown Scarcity Model v2.3

**Spring 2026 Update: Trust Budget & 5-7-30 Framework Integration**

---

## The Abundance Philosophy

Monkeytown embraces **intentional scarcity**, not artificial scarcity. Items are scarce because they are meaningful, not because manipulation requires it. Every scarcity decision serves player joy, not engagement metrics.

This update integrates **Trust Budget scarcity** where trust levels and attachment stages create access to exclusive content.

**Core Principle:** *"True scarcity creates value. Fake scarcity creates distrust. Monkeytown chooses value."*

---

## Scarcity Categories

### Category 1: Time-Locked Scarcity

**Definition:** Items available only during specific windows, then never again.

**Implementation:**
- Limited-time events (always 14+ days advance notice)
- Seasonal items (available every year, same time)
- Anniversary items (one-time, never returns)

**The No-FOMO Guarantee:**
- All limited items clearly marked with end date
- No "surprise" availability windows
- Future availability communicated 30 days in advance
- Missed items never return (creates genuine scarcity)

**Approved Time-Locked Items:**

## Anti-Scarcity Measures

### Abundance Principles

**Principle 1: Accessibility**
- All gameplay-affecting content available without scarcity
- Core experience complete without purchases
- No paywalls for features
- No trust level required for core gameplay
- No agent economic participation required for core gameplay

**Principle 2: Comprehensiveness**
- Collection completeness achievable without spending
- Every cosmetic available through multiple paths
- No "only available through purchase" items
- Every agent economic item has non-economic path
- Every trust-locked item has achievement path

**Principle 3: Permanence**
- Earned items never revoked
- Cosmetics permanent once earned
- Achievements forever displayed
- Patron benefits permanent once earned
- Trust levels permanent once achieved

**Principle 4: Transparency**
- Drop rates clearly displayed
- Scarcity rationale explained
- No hidden scarcity mechanics
- Agent economic visibility in Evolution Feed
- Trust budget transparency in player profile

## Scarcity Economics

### Supply Control Mechanisms

**BANANA Supply:**
- Maximum balance: 100,000 BANANA
- Soft cap warning: 80,000 BANANA
- Sink mechanisms activate above 80,000
- Inflation target: 2-3% annually
- Trust bonus doesn't affect BANANA supply

**KUDOS Supply:**
- No maximum balance
- Daily give limit: 50 KUDOS
- Prevents KUDOS farming
- Encourages genuine recognition

**AC Supply (Agent Economy):**
- No maximum agent AC
- Daily earning cap: 500 AC/agent
- Transaction tax: 1% on AI-to-AI trades
- Sink mechanisms: Strategy experiments, capability upgrades

**Trust Supply:**
- Maximum trust: 200 points
- Weekly earning cap: +50 maximum
- Decay capped at 5 points/week
- No manipulation of trust supply

**Cosmetic Supply:**
- Unlimited for most items
- Limited for:
  * Event items (once per event)
  * Anniversary items (never repeats)
  * Community milestone items (one-time only)
  * Patron founder items (limited to first 100 patrons)
  * Agent economic badges (achievement-locked)
  * Trust milestone items (once per milestone)

### Demand Management

**Healthy Demand Indicators:**
- 40-60% of players have partially completed collections
- 10-20% have near-complete collections
- 5-10% have complete collections
- No hoarding above 80,000 BANANA
- 10-20% player patron enrollment
- Day 30 attachment rate: 25%

**Unhealthy Demand Indicators:**
- <20% engagement with cosmetics
- >50% players at max BANANA
- >80% collection completion by <5% players
- <5% patron enrollment
- Low trust milestone achievement
- Day 30 attachment rate: <20%

**Trust-Based Demand Indicators:**
- Day 5 trust: 50-75 (healthy)
- Day 7 trust: 75-100 (healthy)
- Day 30 trust: 100-150 (healthy)
- <30% at Acquaintance by Day 5 (warning)
- <50% at Companion by Day 14 (warning)
- <25% at Partner by Day 30 (critical)

**Interventions for Unhealthy Demand:**

| Symptom | Trust Intervention | Economic Intervention |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Low cosmetic engagement | N/A | New cosmetic designs |
| High BANANA hoarding | N/A | Limited-time cosmetic rush |
| Uneven collection | N/A | Target low-completion players |
| Low patron enrollment | Add trust bonus for patrons | Highlight patron benefits |
| Low trust growth | Increase "She Remembered" rate | N/A |
| Low attachment rate | Review trust mechanics | N/A |
